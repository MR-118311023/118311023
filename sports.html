Sports

Since a very young age, I have been very interested in sports. My favourite sports are soccer and swimming:

Soccer

I played for my local schoolboy team, Villa F.C. from when I was 9 years old up until I was 17 years and I really enjoyed my time there. I played either central midfield or right wing. I loved training and playing matches and I made loads of friends who were on the team.

I also love watching football. From a very young age, I have supported Arsenal. I absolutely love watching Arsenal play. I feel like they are connected to me. I am over the moon when they win and I feel dejected when they lose.

Swimming

I have always loved swimming. I competed in swimming races when I was younger and more recently, I completed a lifeguard course.

I have always viewed Michael Phelps as a role model. In my opinion, he is the greatest swimmer of all time.
