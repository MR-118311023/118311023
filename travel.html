Travel

England

London

London is by far my favourite destination that I have visited in my life.

It has everything I could possibly want in a city: My favourite football team, Arsenal play there, it has endless tourist sites, an incredible transport system, a lovely culture and overall there is just a great atmosphere there.

Spain

Málaga

Benalmádena, Málaga was the most popular sun destination for my family when I was younger.

We went there nearly every year and I absolutely loved it. It has everything: beautiful beaches, delicious food, amazing water parks, gorgeous sunshine and lovely people.

Portugal

Algarve

I went to Albufeira in the Algarve for my 6th year holiday with my friends and I had one of the best weeks of my life there. The nightlife was absolutely amazing; perfect for young people and the people there are so friendly.

France

Paris

I have visited Paris twice in my life and both times it was an amazing experience. Everything is so beautiful: the food, the sights and the weather. I would love to go back again in the future.
